About Us

Know our story, our mission, and our heart

Our Story

Indian Christian Assembly Ministries was founded with a singular vision: to be a beacon of God's love and truth in our community and beyond. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a thriving congregation united by faith, hope, and love.

We believe in the power of prayer, the authority of Scripture, and the transforming grace of Jesus Christ. Every service, every ministry, and every outreach is driven by our deep commitment to the Great Commission.

Our doors are open to all — regardless of background, culture, or history. You are welcome here.
